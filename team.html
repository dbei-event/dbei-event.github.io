Meet the digital natives behind this amazing initiative..

WESLEY BENN

CHAIRMAN

  

A Sydney-based architect, Wesley is the founder of RTC Events Management. He is a regular speaker at events throughout Australia and overseas talking about technology, facilities management and process change; he founded (and chaired for 5 years) the Revit User Group, Sydney; and is now focused, via his partnership in theDIGITgroup, on SmartCities development worldwide.

RICHARD KUPPUSAMY

SPEAKER MANAGER

  

Richard is a chartered British Architect with more than 15 years of experience in the AEC industry delivering large scale projects in Europe, the Middle East, and Asia regions. At Lendlease Singapore he is Regional Digital Integration Manager APAC responsible for leading digital technology innovation and processes from construction through facility management. He obtained his Masters at Mackintosh School of Architecture.

REXTER RETANA

PARTNERSHIPS MANAGER

  

Rexter is currently the BIM Manager for RSP Architects Planners & Engineers Pte Ltd. He has strong background in Civil & Structural Engineering with a BSc in Civil Engineering and MSc in BIM Management with highest honors. He is currently pursuing a PhD study on the application of Artificial Intelligence, Cognitive Computing and Big Data in construction.

PHIL LAZARUS

MARKETING MANAGER
