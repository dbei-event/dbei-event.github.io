DBEI Night - SG

TOPIC 1

"All-in-One" Modelling

May 25, 2017

Synopsis:

This presentation will provide an in-depth look at how "All-in-One" model approach can improve efficiencies in multi-discipline firm, and how this unique approach can be accomplished with easy tweaks to your Revit Project Teamplates and workflows.

1st Speaker

Mr. Dave Willard

Speaker's Bio:

For the past 25 years, Dave has focused his efforts on the development, implementation, and support of advanced technologies within AEC Industry. As an early of adopter of Revit (1999), Dave has since directed BIM implementation programs for some of the nation’s top Architectural Engineering firms. He continues to be an industry pioneer for Revit/BIM workflows and best practices, specifically for Mega projects ranging from sports arena to water plants.

TOPIC 2

BIM: What does good look like?

DBEI NIght - SG

May 25, 2017

Synopsis:

This presentation will take you through the key principles of determining whether the model information produced is useable. It will highlight the importance of clients defining clear objective requirements and how compliance against these requirements can be frequently monitored.

2nd Speaker

Mr. Alex Nesbitt

Speaker's Bio:

Alex Nesbitt is a BIM Consultant for Turner & Townsend. His role is both technical and strategic in nature, focusing on the implementation and management of BIM processes on major project programmes. He is also a qualified Architectural Technologist who previously worked with National Building Specification (NBS) UK for the development of Uniclass 2015, BIM toolkit and National BIM Library.
